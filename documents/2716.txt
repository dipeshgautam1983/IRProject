UofM Online Programs Orientation Online Readiness Resources About AtoZ List Home UofM Online Online Programs at the University of Memphis. Welcome to UofM Online! We are proud to offer you an online master's program ranked as one of the top programs in the nation in 2014-2015 by ACO (Affordable Colleges Online). You can pursue your college degree and manage your busy lifestyle by enrolling in one of the online undergraduate or graduate degree programs available at the University of Memphis. Online programs provide flexibility so you can succeed at earning a degree in spite of family commitments, erratic work schedules, or limited mobility. Each of our online courses are offered via the Web, so wherever there is a high speed Internet connection, you can access your class and the many resources available to you as a student at a major university! Our programs are accredited through the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. Interested in pursuing your degree online? We offer a large selection of fully online undergraduate and graduate degree programs for the dedicated distance student. Use the menu to browse descriptions of each of the online programs or request more information about programs of interest. Out-of-state students: Please read this information on state authorization before applying to an online degree program. Questions About the Online Learning Experience? Check out the Online Student Orientation or use the menu to the left to locate additional information, such as advising and admissions procedures for online students, tuition costs, and financial aid.
