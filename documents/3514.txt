Dual Admissions Program (DAP) The Dual Admissions Program (DAP) is a program that allows students attending participating community colleges in the Tennessee Board of Regents (TBR) system to transfer to the University of Memphis upon completion of the Associate's Degree (A.A., A.S., and A.S.T.). Successful completion of all DAP requirements guarantees acceptance to the University of Memphis. The following schools are currently participating in DAP: Motlow State Community College Northeast State Community College Visit the DAP web site to find detailed information on requirements, participation, and enrollment forms. Partnership Enrollment Program (PEP) PEP is a similar program to DAP, but it ecompasses participation by more regional community colleges in the mid-south. Successful completion of all PEP requirements guarantees admission to the University of Memphis. The following regional community colleges participate in PEP: Dyersburg State Community College Jackson State Community College Mid South Community College Southwest Tennessee Community College Visit the PEP web site to find more information and enrollment forms. Dual Enrollment (DE) If you are a current high school student and would like to earn college credit while completing requirements for your high school diploma, the University of Memphis offers DE to students attending participating area high schools. This opportunity can help students to earn credit for certain courses while they are in high school. Please visit the Dual Enrollment website for more information.
