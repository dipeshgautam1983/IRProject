Directions First time students and prospective students will find the University directions helpful. Instead of turning on Patterson, from Central look for the Central parking lot, which is the largest general parking closest to Dunn Hall. If you are a visiting professor or guest of the department, please use the directions to the University Holiday Inn and Fogelman Executive Conference Center &amp; Hotel .
