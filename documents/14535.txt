Department of Political Science About Undergraduate Graduate JD/MA Program Internships People People Bios Awards and Kudos Home Political Science People Awards and Kudos Awards and Kudos Several faculty members and students in the Political Science department have recently received awards. We congratulate all of the following on their accomplishments. Dr. Sharon Stanley- Dunavant Professorship (The Dunavant Professorship recognizes exceptional achievement in teaching, scholarship, service, and outreach.) Dr. Leah Windsor- Minerva Research Award (This grant, the Minerva Research Initiative, is sponsored by the U.S. Department of Defense and focuses on areas of strategic importance to U.S. national security policy.) Dr. Eric Groenendyk- Hooks Institute Grant (For his research project on "Extremism in Defense of Liberty: The Effect of Ideology and Prospect for Social Change in Contemporary American Politics.") Dr. Matthias Kaelberer- University of Memphis Honors Program Excellence in Undergraduate Mentoring Award Dr. Nicole Detraz- Alumni Association Distinguished Teaching Award Recent and forthcoming faculty publications: Stanley, Sharon (forthcoming 2015) "The Enduring Challenge of Racial Integration in the United States."
