How to Back up a Computer | The University of Memphis Center for Teaching and Learning The University of Memphis Center for Teaching and Learning Explore, Collaborate, and Learn Search Menu Main menu Skip to primary content Home About CTL CTL Mission Facutly Resources Faculty Workshops Student Resources eCourseware Technology Coaches Learning Technologies for Tigers Post navigation &larr; Previous Next &rarr; How to Back up a Computer Posted on June 24, 2015 by Jessica Johnson One way to ensure that you never lose any of your important files is to back up your computer regularly. Online storage services can be costly, but there are a few ways to back up your computer for free. Windows Click the Start Button. Click Control Panel. Under System and Security, click Back up your computer. Follow the steps in the wizard. Mac Connect your external drive to your mac. Click Use As Backup Disk. Under System Preferences, click Time Machine. Follow the instructions. Tips Your storage device should be twice the size of the hard drive you back up Make sure the backup process is complete before using your computer. Keep your storage devices in a safe and secure place. Set a time for automatic backups. Make a backup of your backup.
