Prior to her PhD, she received a BA in philosophy from St. Anselm College and an MA in philosophy from the University of South Carolina. Her research and teaching interests include philosophy of mind, social epistemology, and social ontology. Her work has appeared in such journals as Episteme, Review of Philosophy and Psychology, Midwest Studies in Philosophy, Philosophical Explorations, and Philosophy of the Social Sciences. Professor Tollefsen's book Groups as Agents has been published with Polity Press. Recent Publications Tollefsen, D. 2015. Groups as Agents, Polity Press . Tollefsen, D., Kruez, R. &amp; Dale, R.
