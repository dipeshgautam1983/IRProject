About Blog About Blog The Blog tool is a way to share information on topics that interest you. You can post and respond to questions, engage in discussions, and share opinions and comments with other users. Blog entries can be public or private. The Blog tool has two main areas: My Blog for creating and maintaining your own blog Blog Watch for reading other users’ blogs. Access the Blog tool Click the Blog link on your course navigation bar.
