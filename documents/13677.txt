Not only do computers contain personal information, they also contain potentially hazardous materials. Here are a few ways that you can dispose of your computer safely Note Before you dispose of your computer it is important to back up all of your data. Using data wiping software will delete the contents of your hard drive. Click here to learn more about backing up your computer. Cleaning you Hard Drive You cannot clean your hard drive by simply deleting files. It is easy for these deleted files to be recovered using a data recovery program. The only way to permanently remove data is to overwrite and wipe your hard drive. There are a variety of free programs that you can use to overwrite your hard drive. For more information about overwriting you hard drive click here . Now What? There is more than one way to dispose of your computer. First, there are many benefits to recycling your computer and it very easy to do. Also, many retailers offer both online and in-store recycling. If you do not recycle your computer, you may decide to donate or resell it. You can find more information about computer disposal on the FTC Consumer Information page .
