University of Memphis Lambuth Classes Admissions Campus Life Residence Student Services Campus Services Home UofM Lambuth Campus Life The best combination of serious and fun. Our students are always focused on their academics, but we have fun along the way. Lots of it. Outside of the classroom, this intimate campus is a vibrant, fun place to be—made up of diverse groups. You will live, play and connect in a classic college environment, with Fraternity and Sorority Affairs, club sports, movies, cool concerts and other special events tailored to appeal to UofM Lambuth students. We keep campus life interesting and exciting.
