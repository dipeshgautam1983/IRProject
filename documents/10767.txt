Fogelman College - Department of Business Information and Technology Welcome About Admissions Programs Courses Faculty Research FCBE Programs Degree Programs Certificate Programs Research Programs Home BIT Programs MSBA Program MSBA Program Admissions Course Requirements Business Information and Technology (BIT) is the application of information technologies, including computers and communications networks, to business. A BIT degree appeals to students who wish to combine technology, business management, and communication skills in leadership positions. Top BIT jobs and salaries: Systems Engineer IT Project Manager Computer/Network Security Consultant Intelligence Analyst Software Developer Business Analyst, IT Source: CNNMoney.com Depending on type of job and geographic location ~ $45K - $100K or more.
