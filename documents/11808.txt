Skalli (Biology, IMC Director), Bumgardner (Bioengineering), Lindner (Bioengineering), Mishra (Physics) and Larsen (Earth Sciences).The Nova NanoSEM 650 is a Schottky Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscope (FE-SEM) that combines high and low voltage ultra high resolution capabilities at low vacuum. The instrument features a two-mode final lens, beam deceleration and environmental SEM technology.
