You Can Donate to the IEAA - The Institute of Egyptian Art and Archaeology (IEAA), a Tennessee Center of Excellence, is funded in part by the University of Memphis and in part by the State of Tennessee/TBR. In this time of decreased funding for public institutions, the IEAA needs your help to continue its tradition of excellence in community education, teaching, research, and exhibition. To make a donation to the IEAA or to see a list of sponsored projects you can help support, click here. To Donate to the IEAA Donations can be made by check or credit card - If writing a check , please mail it directly to the University of Memphis Foundation at the following address: University of Memphis Foundation P.O. Box 1000 Dept. 238 The University of Memphis Memphis, TN 38148 Please make your check payable to the University of Memphis Foundation - #0695 . Specify that you are supporting the IEAA by writing IEAA or Egyptian Institute in the "comment" or "memo" line on your check. It is critical that you include the name of the IEAA on your check. If you do not, your donation will go to the University of Memphis General Fund. We THANK YOU for your support! Donations are tax deductible to the full extent allowed by law. For questions, contact the IEAA at 901.678.2555.
