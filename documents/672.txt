Testing Center About Services Accommodations Visitor Information Lambuth Testing Services Educational Testing Service ACT Residual COMPASS CLEP DSST Miller Analogies Test Oral Proficiency Interview Para Pro Assessment Proctor Services TEAS Home Testing Center Services CLEP CLEP (COLLEGE LEVEL EXAMINATION PROGRAM) The College-Level Examination Program® (CLEP) offers you the opportunity to receive college credit while saving time and money. Depending on your University, examinees are able to earn qualifying scores on up to 33 introductory-level college subject examinations. CLEP exams are approximately 90 minute exams, which cost $80 each. The College Level Examination Program (CLEP) is a computerized exam that is offered on a walk-in basis and is administered in room 111 of the Brister Hall/ Wilder Tower complex. The CLEP administration fee is $25.00 and is offered between the hours of 8:30 am – 1:30 pm on the dates specified below (unless otherwise specified on the calendar). CLEP REGISTRATION PROCEDURES: Create your personal account and pay the $80.00 exam fee through the CLEP My Account registration portal. The registration platform will allow you to register for a CLEP exam 24/7, manage your personal information any time prior to taking the exam, select your score recipient institution, and purchase study materials. Submit $25 registration fee through our convenient online payment system (Note: Exam fees must be paid through the online payment system). Prepare for exam (center recommendations): InstantCert , Research Education Association and Petersons . On the day of test, please report to Brister Hall Room 111: Have your printed UofM payment receipt (without a printed proof of payment, examinees will not be admitted into the exam and all applicable fees will be forfeited.) Have your proof of purchase "Voucher Number" (provided by CLEP) One form of ID: Primary : Valid government-issued with name, photo and signature Home-schooled or under 21 with no valid government-issued ID : College Board ID Form (found on CLEP website) CLEP score reports will be available immediately following the exam (exception: Exams that include an essay portion) November 2015 December 2015 January 2016 Mon. 11/16 Tue. 12/1 TBA Tue. 11/24 Mon. 12/7 Mon. 11/30 NOTE: Each institution of higher education will have individual requirements that can be attained by contacting the particular school.
