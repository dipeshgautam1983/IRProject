Study Abroad Programs Students J-1 Students Faculty Funding Resources News About Media &amp; News Important Dates Newsletter Videos Photo Contest Global Race Home Study Abroad Media &amp; News Media &amp; News The 2015 Photo Contest Theme is "Engaging with the Host Community &amp; Culture" Vote for the 2016 Tigers Abroad Photo Contest People's Choice Award Entries will be judged on the ability to convey a cross-cultural experience through "Engaging with Host Community &amp; Culture" in a single photograph. This can be defined as personal interaction with the local population of the host country and/or the portrayal of the essence of your international travel experience. Entries will be accepted from University of Memphis (U of M) students, alumni, faculty, and staff who have had an international experience within the last year. University Photos of landscapes will be accepted, however, we prefer that you submit photos that exhibit cultural immersion in a country outside the United States or in Memphis if you are an international student. Participants may only enter one photo and the photo must be taken by the individual. All photos should be accompanied with a brief text describing the cross-cultural component of the photo. Submissions will be judged by a committee. Travel vouchers will be awarded to the 1st, 2nd and 3rd place winners for each category: 1st: $100 Visa Gift Card 2nd: $75 Visa Gift Card 3rd: $50 Visa Gift Card Please Submit a digital jpeg file of the photo to the online application by the deadline with a completed entry form. Entries will be on display at the Ned R. McWherter Library Rotunda during International Education Week, November 16-20. The deadline to submit an entry has been extended to Monday, November 9th.
