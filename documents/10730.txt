Home - Gaming - LibGuides at University of Memphis Libraries University Libraries LibGuides Gaming Home Gaming: Home Gaming resources at the University of Memphis Home Research Games Equipment Gift Policy Gaming at the University of Memphis The University of Memphis Libraries are committed to providing resources across disciplines for the benefit of students in our community. It is essential that students have access to the technology they need to succeed in any discipline. The gaming resources at the U of M are available to all students. Circulation Rules EVERYTHING YOU NEED IS HERE We circulate games as well as equipment. Everything can be checked out for 4 hours. Only One Game, Controller, and Headset per ID Game On ! We have two consoles available in the Technology Sandbox; the WiiU and the XBoxONE. Controllers, GamePad, and headsets can be checked out at the McWherter Library circulation desk.
