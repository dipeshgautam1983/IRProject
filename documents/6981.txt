Mission, vision and values Our Mission The College of Communication and Fine Arts has four distinct aims: I. To prepare students for careers in architecture, design, fine arts, communication, mass media, and the performing arts II. To offer broad exposure in the arts and communication to students throughout the university III. To promote research and creative activity on the part of the faculty IV. To enrich the cultural life of the University, the City of Memphis, and the Mid-South region through public programs, exhibitions, performances, community engagement and partnerships. Our Vision The College of Communication and Fine Arts at the University of Memphis will build on its reputation as a rich and dynamic environment where creativity, culture, and ideas flourish and as fertile ground for the artists, scholars, architects and innovators of the future. Our Values The College of Communication and Fine Arts and its constituent departments embrace the following values: Creativity and critical thinking Access and diversity Community engagement and partnership Sense of place Pedagogical excellence Innovation Interdisciplinary research and collaboration.
