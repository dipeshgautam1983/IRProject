UofM Online Programs Orientation Online Readiness Resources About AtoZ List Programs Undergraduate Graduate Home UofM Online Programs MS in School Library Information Specialist Master of Science in School Library Information Specialist Our Master of Science degree program with School Library Information Specialist endorsement from the Department of Instruction and Curriculum Leadership at the University of Memphis is committed to developing school librarians/media specialists who are inspired to engage in critical thinking, problem solving, and lifelong learning. Completion of our secondary school librarian/media specialist program signifies that candidates for licensure have developed knowledge and skills in pedagogy, research skills, and information technology. This program is designed for students who are seeking advanced study and professional development in education focusing on a School Library Information Specialist Endorsement only, or the Master of Science degree with this endorsement. The Master of Science in Instruction and Curriculum Leadership with School Library Information Specialist endorsement is available to individuals with or without teacher licensure who desire to work in education-related settings in the capacity as a school library information media specialist. The program provides students with the opportunity to expand their knowledge base in teaching areas and areas related to education. The online format provides accessibility and flexibility while delivering a rigorous learning experience over the Internet for each student at a reasonable cost.
