Fee Payment: Click on your Account$ tab View Fee Payment deadlines and Refund dates (both under "Dates and Deadlines"). View/Pay Fees. NO PAYMENT = NO CLASSES If you have not satisfied fees by the published fee payment deadline, your classes will be deleted the evening prior to the term's begin date. Beginning with the Late Registration Period, you will be able to reregister for classes that have available seats, but you will be assessed a $100 late registration fee in addition to regular tuition and fees. If you do not satisfy fees by the end of the late registration period, you will be assessed a $100 late payment fee. If your fees remain unpaid at the close of business on the day AFTER the end of the late registration period, your classes will be deleted. If you decide not to attend, you must withdraw from all classes on Student Self Service prior to the first day of classes for the part-of-term (session) to ensure that you will not owe fees. Warning - Dropping courses may impact Hope Lottery Scholarship and/or Financial Aid eligibility. Your Privacy Rights - Annual FERPA Notification.
