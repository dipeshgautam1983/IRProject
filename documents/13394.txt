Master of Arts (M.A.) in Speech-Language Pathology Please familiarize yourself with all items on this page before submitting your application. Applications for the professional degree programs (AuD and MA) are due by February 1 each year. Students applying to the professional degree programs must Complete the Communication Sciences &amp; Disorders Centralized Application Service (CSDCAS) common application AND Submit a formal application to The University of Memphis. Please note that official GRE scores must be sent separately to CSDCAS (Code 1756) and to The University of Memphis (Code 1459) using two different ETS codes. CSDCAS APPLICATION The professional degree programs (AuD and MA) in the School of Communication Sciences and Disorders use a centralized application known as the Communication Sciences &amp; Disorders Centralized Application Service (CSDCAS). Students applying for the Fall 2016 entering class should apply online using the CSDCAS application. You can access the CSDCAS portal here . Your CSDCAS application must be complete by February 1, 2016 . A completed application means that all letters of recommendation and personal statements have been received and transcripts and GRE scores have been verified. The CSDCAS portal will close on this date and no additional applications will be accepted. Applications will be reviewed as soon as the transcripts are verified by CSDCAS and letters of recommendation and transcripts are received by CSDCAS. Official GRE scores are required for admission, and must be submitted on the CSDCAS application portal (Code 1756) and to the University of Memphis (Code (1459). You are strongly advised to begin the process of arranging for transcripts, letters and GRE scores prior to November 1, 2015. APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS Complete the CSDCAS Application Transcripts: Submit official transcripts from all universities you have attended to the CSDCAS portal. You will be prompted to download a form that requests official transcripts from each college or university you have attended. You will also be prompted to enter individual classes you have taken on the CSDCAS portal. Both steps need to be completed. GRE Scores: Submit official GRE scores to the CSDCAS portal using the CSDCAS program code 1756. Letters of Recommendation: Submit 3 letters of recommendation from faculty or other individuals who can attest to your ability to be successful as a graduate student. You will be prompted by the CSDCAS portal to enter the names of individuals who will write your recommendation letters. This will generate an email to those individuals who will upload their letters directly to CSDCAS. The letters of recommendation should be completed using the CSDCAS reference request form including the ratings grid and an uploaded written letter. These comments are a critical part of the review process because we are very interested in your letter writers’ opinions about your potential for graduate study and clinical work. You will list your references as “Evaluations” in the “Supporting Information” section of CSDCAS. Personal Statement: This statement gives you an opportunity to share your personal goals and information about yourself and why you are interested in our program. The personal statement is uploaded under “Documents.” Begin uploading documentation to your CSDCAS application early so that all of your information is complete no later than February 1, 2016. Regularly check your CSDCAS portal to monitor your application and the receipt of all supporting documents. IT IS YOUR RESPONSIBILITY TO MONITOR THE PORTAL AND INSURE THAT NO DOCUMENTS ARE MISSING. Complete a formal (supplemental) application directly with the University of Memphis Graduate School Submit a formal application to the University of Memphis Graduate School by clicking here . You will need to send official GRE scores (program code = 1459) and transcripts to the University of Memphis in addition to sending them to CSDCAS. YOU DO NOT NEED TO SEND LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION OR PERSONAL STATEMENTS TO THE UNIVERSITY OF MEMPHIS; LETTERS OF RECOMMENDATION AND PERSONAL STATEMENTS SHOULD BE UPLOADED DIRECTLY THROUGH THE CSDCAS PORTAL. CHECKLIST FOR YOUR CSDCAS APPLICATION: Review all of the instructions on the CSDCAS site . By November 1st, arrange for transcripts, GRE scores, and letters of recommendation to be sent directly to CSDCAS. The University of Memphis CSDCAS program code is 1756. Submit your completed CSDCAS application before February 1, 2016. Regularly check your CSDCAS portal to monitor your application and the receipt of all supporting documents.
