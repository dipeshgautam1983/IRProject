Evans Home School of Communication Sciences and Disorders News and Events Outstanding Alumni Award Outstanding Alumni Award The University of Memphis School of Communication Sciences and Disorders Alumni Chapter seeks nominations for its annual Outstanding Alumnus Award. The Chapter will present this award to a deserving CSD graduate at the annual Mid-South Conference on Communicative Disorders. Graduates who demonstrate outstanding achievement in academic pursuits, research activities or community involvement are eligible for this award. To nominate an individual, detailed information is needed regarding the nominee's history, resume, honors received, published works and two letters of support. Additionally, any other related materials would be helpful. Members of the CSD Alumni Chapter Board of Directors and CSD teaching and clinical faculty are not eligible. The deadline for nominations is May 1st each year. Previous Award Winners 2015: Ann Burlison, M.A. 2014: Darlene Winters, M.A. 2013: David Zapala, Ph.D. 2012: Tracey Baldwin, M.A. 2011: Kim S.
