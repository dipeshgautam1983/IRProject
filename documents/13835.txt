Division of Student Affairs About Us Departments For Students For Faculty &amp; Staff For Parents Give Back Home Division of Student Affairs Faculty &amp; Staff Pyramid Awards Pyramid Awards Presented to one hourly paid and one monthly paid employee of the Division, the annual Pyramid Award is awarded to employees who have achieved the highest standard of service and deserve to be recognized for consistently striving at an exceptional level to reach the pinnacle of the Pyramid. A nominated employee must: Be a permanent full-time member in the division at the time of nomination and selection as a winner Have been employed in the division at least one (1) year at the time of the nomination Not have won the award in the previous five (5) years. For a list of past winners, please click here . Assistant Vice Presidents, Associate Vice Presidents and Vice President are not eligible to receive the award Guidelines to consider in nomination of employee are: Consistently performs above and beyond the call of duty Displays consistent dedication to tasks Provides exemplary customer service to students, faculty, and staff Promotes fair play, cooperation, and diversity within the division and University Supports University activities and goals Makes efforts to support collaboration and coordination within the division and the University Has developed ideas and project/system improvements which have improved divisional or University processes and/or services Nominations should be made by those in the Division of Student Affairs Employee will receive: $150 Gift Basket Pyramid Award List of Past Winners Contact us Let us know how we may assist you Organizational Chart Learn about our division's organizational structure Mission and Goals Learn about what drives us Full sitemap Admissions Prospective Students Undergraduate Graduate Law School International Parents Scholarship &amp;
