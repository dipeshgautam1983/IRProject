Department of Mathematical Sciences About People Undergraduate Graduate Research News Undergraduate Admissions Honors Program Accelerated BS/MS (ABM) Advising Scholarships Courses &amp; Syllabi Cantor Sect Home Math Sciences Undergraduate Courses and Syllabi Courses &amp; Syllabi Official course descriptions can be found at the university's Undergraduate Catalog . Current students should look up classes via the Student Tab within the MyMemphis portal to view and register for classes; prospective students can view semester course listings via the University's class schedule search . If you know the instructor's name, then you can find syllabi for their courses as well as their teaching schedule on their individual webpages . Undergraduate &amp; Graduate Course Map Below are maps of mathematics, statistics, and teaching mathematics courses at both the undergraduate and graduate levels with arrows designating each course's prerequisites and corequisites. These maps are helpful in visualizing which classes must be taken before others in progressing towards an undergraduate or graduate degree in mathematical sciences, however students should always refer to the official undergraduate and graduate catalogs for the most up to date information. Note: Moving your mouse over the courses will display detailed information about each course. Some courses have links to representative syllabi . Note that syllabi may vary between sections of the same course. You can also download a printable version of this map .
