Conference &amp; Event Services Facilities Conference Services Internal Scheduling Contact Us Conference Services Accommodations Conference Housing Dining &amp; Catering Home Conference &amp; Event Services Conference Services Accommodations Accommodations FedEx Institute of Technology, a $23 million, state-of-the-art facility like no other. The 190 seat tiered amphitheater boasting the second largest implementation of digital congress units outside the United Nations, is complimented by an 85-seat high definition presentation theater and 17 meeting rooms. Large projection screens, web cams, touch panel screens, laptop computers, totally wireless network, SIM cards, poly-vision and video teleconferencing, and interactive white boards are just some of the cutting-edge features of this facility. Fogelman Executive Conference Center &amp; Hotel sets the benchmark for meeting planner expectation. This International Association of Conference Centers approved full-service meeting facility offers 51 queen guest rooms, three amphitheaters: 2,580 sq ft. amphitheater, 3,572 sq ft theater, 1,425 theater, 11 conference rooms, and two dining rooms. Over 17,000 sq ft. of meeting space is available. Our award winning hotel, with its four story atrium of rich woods and leather seating, offers 82 two-rooms suites, a restaurant, private dining room, lounge, five meeting rooms and a 12,180 sq. ft. grand ballroom. Over 14,000 sq. ft. of meeting space is available. Conference housing accommodations are available from May through August for both on- and off-campus groups seeking affordable housing, excellent dining services, and a variety of meeting areas located both indoors and outdoors on our campus. Richardson Towers is the PRIMARY location for conference housing on the University of Memphis Campus. Click Here for additional information and conference housing application.
