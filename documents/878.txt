School Psychology - June 15 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Applying THERE ARE TWO REQUIRED STEPS: Apply to the Department of Psychology Apply to the University of Memphis Graduate School --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- *** Step 1: Apply to the Department of Psychology *** To complete the online Department application you will need to be ready to: Supply e-mail addresses for three recommenders who will be automatically notified after application is submitted. Letters will be accepted up to 10 days after the application deadline. Report your GPA and GRE scores. Supply names of up to 3 University of Memphis faculty you are interested in working with. Provide a maximum 1200 word Research Interests and Professional Goals statement. Click on Department Application under Apply to Psychology Graduate Programs in the column to the left to access the Department Application. -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- *** Step 2: Apply to the University of Memphis Graduate School *** To complete your application to the University of Memphis Graduate School, you must: Send or arrange to have sent: A non-refundable fee ($35 domestic, $60 international) Official transcripts Official GRE test scores (Verbal, Quantitative, Analytic) TOEFL score if applicable Send above items to: Graduate Admissions The University of Memphis 101 Wilder Tower Memphis, TN 38152-3370 Complete the online Graduate School Application. Click here "Apply Now" to access the Graduate School Application -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Both steps ( University and Department Applications ) must be completed and all material received for an application to be fully reviewed. -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Please direct questions to Cynthia Walker, Graduate Secretary Department of Psychology 901.678.4340 cywshngt@memphis.edu Graduate Clinical About the Program Clinical Faculty Child Clinical Clinical Health Psychology Psychotherapy Research Admission Clinical Student Handbook Clinical Forum Schedule Experimental About the Program Experimental Faculty Cognitive Behavioral Neuroscience Required Courses Admission Financial Support School About the Programs School Faculty Child &amp;
