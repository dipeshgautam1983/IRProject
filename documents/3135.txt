University College About Undergraduate Graduate Resources Online Home University College Resources Scheduling an Advising Appointment Scheduling an Advising Appointment New students must start an account. Remember how you enter your U-Number as this will be your permanent Log In. (For example, if you use a capital 'U', it will not remember you if you use a lower case 'U' or no 'U' at all the next time you try to log-in) Once you are registered, follow these instructions. 1. Select your Major. If you are an Undergraduate Student, choose "University College". If you are a Graduate Student, choose "University College Graduate Studies". 2. Select an appointment type of either Office, Skype or Phone. 3. Select your assigned advisor. Sara Williams: Student last name starts with A - E Ernest Hendley: Student last name starts with F, G, H, S Dot Hale: Student last name starts with I - O Mary Brignole: Student last name starts with P - R, T - Z Mary Crites: (University College Online Only) Keith Sisson: Graduate Students (MALS and MPS degrees) 4. Choose an available date on the calendar, to view the first five (5) available appointment times. Choose the time that works best for your schedule. Please note: The system does not allow same day or next day appointments and you will not be able to schedule an appointment more than 4 weeks out. 5. You can choose to receive an email or a text message appointment confirmation as well as appointment reminders! If you do not receive a confirmation message, go back into the system and be sure your appointment was made correctly. Appointment Plus is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week! You can cancel or reschedule appointments by accessing your Appointments Plus account. Note: If you set up an appointment with the wrong advisor, we’ll change it to your assigned advisor if the same day and time is available, or email you requesting you reschedule with the correct advisor. Request More Information Complete our online form for additional degree information. Online Appointments Academic Advising is available in-person, by phone and web conference.
