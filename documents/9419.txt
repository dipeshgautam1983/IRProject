Help us continue to provide you with excellent parent and family services with a donation to the Parent Fund. Each donation helps to continue the services that we provide to the parents and family members of University of Memphis students and increase our efforts to help you help your student. Thank you in advance for your support of the University of Memphis Parent &amp; Family Services! Giving Online To make an online gift to the Parent Fund, click here . Your browser will display the online giving form. Complete the information requested to make your gift. Giving by Check Make your check payable to the University of Memphis Foundation. Please include the words "Parent Fund" on the check memo line. Mail your check to: Department 238 The UofM Foundation P.O. Box 1000 Memphis, TN 38148-0001 Or personally deliver your check to the Alumni Center at 635 Normal Street. Office hours are Monday - Friday, 8:00 am to 4:30 pm Get your FAMILY T-shirt Donate $30 or more to the Parent Fund and receive a FAMILY t-shirt. To get your t-shirt, donate per the instructions above and then email parents@memphis.edu with your donation amount, your mailing address and the size t-shirt that you would like. We have sizes adult Medium to 2XL. Please Note: To receive items, donations must be designated to the Parent Fund and can not be designated to another UofM department, college or area.
