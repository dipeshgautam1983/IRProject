Graduate School Future Students Current Students Faculty/Staff Programs Events Resources Faculty and Staff Curricular Procedures Curricular Revision Forms TBR Academic Program Proposal Policies TBR Academic Program Proposal Guidelines TBR Academic Program Proposal Forms Graduate School Calendar Graduate School Forms Graduate School Contact Information Home Graduate School Faculty/Staff School of Nursing Loewenberg School of Nursing The University bestows the designation "Graduate Faculty" upon faculty following review of their credentials and recommendation by their colleagues. The University maintains six levels of graduate faculty: (1) Full, (2) Associate, (3) Adjunct, (4) Adjunct Research Co-Mentor, (5) Affiliate, and (6) Adjunct Teaching. Only Full graduate faculty members may chair doctoral committees. Adjunct Research Co-Mentor members may serve as co-chair of a master's or doctoral committee. Full or Associate graduate faculty may chair master's committees. Only one adjunct or affiliate graduate faculty member may serve as a voting member on any master's or doctoral committee. Teaching adjunct members may not serve on graduate committees. Full MEMBERS L.
