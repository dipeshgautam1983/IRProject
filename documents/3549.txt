One of the fundamental objectives to consider is how you will prepare yourself for a successful career. To help you plan for the future, below are a few points you should apply to your college search process in the early years of high school to help you toward the end. 1. Stay on track and strive to do the best you can in all your high school 2. Identify your strengths and determine what career path fits those strengths 3. Narrow your career choices and establish goals necessary to discover a career path As you approach your high school graduation, the Office of Admissions welcomes your inquiries and invites you to explore the resources and academic majors offered by the University of Memphis.
