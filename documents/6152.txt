Do Black Patent Leather Shoes Really Reflect Up? By John R. Powers Music &amp; Lyrics by James Quinn &amp; Alaric Jans Nov. 7-9 and 14-16, 2013 PHOTOS Sex education, confession, nuns, heartache and first love – all ingredients for a fun filled night at the theatre – and all part of growing up in 1950s Catholic school. This delightful musical captures the comedy, the horror, joy and tenderness of adolescent growing pains. Eddie Ryan and his friends journey from 2nd grade to graduation in this hilarious and poignant musical. It will tickle the funny bone of anyone who ever went to school, public or private!
