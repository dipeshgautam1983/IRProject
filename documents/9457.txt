Fogelman College - Department of Economics Welcome Programs Faculty Research News FCBE About Programs Faculty Research News Home ECON Faculty MICHAEL GOOTZEIT MICHAEL GOOTZEIT Professor, Department of Economics Phone 901.678.4625 Email mgoot@memphis.edu Fax 901.678.2685 Office BA 419 Office Hours Tu, Th: 9:45-11am and by appointment Website Biography Dr. Michael Gootzeit, Professor, research interests include the history of monetary theory, savings, and rent in relation to classical, neo-classical, and modern economics. He received his BS in Mathematics in 1961 from City College, New York; his MS and Ph.D. degrees from Purdue in 1963 and 1966, respectively. He was named a Krannert Fellow from 1964-1966, and was a National Defense Fellow from 1961-1964 at Purdue University.
