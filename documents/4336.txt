Fogelman College - Customer-Driven MBA Welcome How to Apply Pre-Screening Curriculum FAQs FCBE Home CDMBA Welcome The Customer-Driven MBA program is the nation’s only full-time Corporate Sponsored MBA program that is uniquely designed to emphasize specific industry needs. By partnering with Memphis-based corporations, the Fogelman College of Business &amp; Economics can offer students the opportunity to obtain an industry-centered graduate education with assistantships and possible full-time employment upon successful completion of the degree. AACSB Accredited Program – Standard achieved by only 5% business schools world-wide A 36 credit – hour, 17 month Fall to Fall program Direct work experience with sponsoring company during the program paying a stipend of $27,000 over the 17-month period Possible employment with the sponsoring corporation immediately after graduation for a competitive salary in a highly-desired position for a minimum of 2 years post-graduation 3 different tracks to choose from: Marketing, Biomedical and Entrepreneurship To be considered: 1-2 years of professional experience in a marketing related field We highly recommend candidates have at least a 310 GRE or equivalent GMAT score. Legally eligible to work in the United States Bachelor’s degree or higher at an accredited institution Class Profile Class of 2015 and 2016 Average GMAT 600 Average Work Experience 4 yrs Average GPA 3.37 Average Age 29 yrs Male Enrollment 56% Female Enrollment 44% How To Apply What's your first step?
