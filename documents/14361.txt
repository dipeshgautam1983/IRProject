Over the course of the semester, the first three rows in the center will be reserved for the project at the times indicated below. Students not involved in the project may still use the rest of the center. We apologize for any inconvenience. THE HOURS FOR THE STUDY WILL BE POSTED IN THE CENTER AND HERE EVERY WEEK. Week of Sept 20 Tue, Sept 22: 12:30-3:00 p.m., 5:30-7:30 p.m. Wed, Sept. 23: 8:30 a.m.-1:00 p.m. Thu, Sept 24: 7:30 a.m.-1:30 p.m., 6:30-9:00 p.m. Fri, Sept 25: 7:30 a.m.-1:00 p.m. Placement Testing Information: Placement tests are available on a walk-in basis for French, German, Japanese, and Spanish. You must arrive at least one hour before the Language Media Center closes to begin any placement exam. Results are provided immediately upon completion of the exam. Tests in Italian and Latin are available by appointment only. If you need to make an appointment for testing in Italian or in Latin, or if you have any questions about anything else, please call the Department of Foreign Languages.
