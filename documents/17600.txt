Arts &amp; Sciences E-Files Newsletter Headlines Announcements Kudos College Events Department Seminars Home E-Files Announcements Announcements Works in Progress Symposium will spotlight undergraduate research The Works in Progress Symposium (WIPS) will be held Monday, Nov. 9, from noon to 5 p.m. in the University Center (River Room, Bluff Room, Poplar Room, Memphis B and Shelby Room). This annual event provides a means for undergraduate students throughout the University to share their research with the general University community, and recognizes the significant contribution to research by UofM undergraduates. This year 47 students will be participating in the symposium featuring presentations of research and creative projects from many different fields including anthropology, art, biology, biomedical engineering, chemistry, dance, dietetics, English, health and sport sciences, hospitality and resort management, psychology and sociology. Sponsored by the Helen Hardin Honors Program, WIPS is free and open for everyone to attend. For more information, contact the Honors Program Office at honors@memphis.edu or 678-2690. Employee Charitable Giving Campaign ends Nov. 13 The annual Employee Charitable Giving Campaign is underway and runs through Friday, Nov. 13. Contributions made during the campaign benefit three federated philanthropic organizations and the agencies they represent, plus a group of independent agencies. The University's goal this year is $50,000 in pledges. More information on the campaign is available at . Employees can make a pledge, establish a donation to a charity of their choice through payroll deduction, or make a one-time gift. For any questions, contact Human Resources at hr@memphis.edu.
