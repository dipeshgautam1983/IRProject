HIAD Program Policy on Online Fees Online Fees Online fees ($100/credit hour) apply to all online courses. If you are in a program that includes both face - to - face and online courses, you will pay an online fee for an online class and regular tuition for face - to - face courses. E - Rate The eRate ( an out - of - state student rate) fee is assessed to students who are classified as non - residents of the State of Tennessee and enrolled in all online courses .
