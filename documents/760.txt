Testing Center About Services Accommodations Visitor Information Lambuth Testing Services Educational Testing Service ACT Residual COMPASS CLEP DSST Miller Analogies Test Oral Proficiency Interview Para Pro Assessment Proctor Services TEAS Home Testing Center Services Para Pro Assessment Para Pro Assessment The ParaPro Assessment is a computerized exam that measures the reading, writing and basic math competencies of practicing and prospective paraprofessionals. In accordance with the No Child Left Behind Act, the ParaPro Assessment can be used to certify teacher's aides and assistants in the instruction of these subjects to school children. Registration Information Cost: $35 Test Administration Fee - payable through our secure online payment system + a $55 Exam Fee - payable on test day by Credit/Debit card that displays the MasterCard, Visa or American Express logo - all testing fees are non-refundable &amp; non-transferable. Examinees are to report to Brister Hall 112 at least 15 minutes prior to your testing time. Please be prompt. Late arrivals will not be admitted. Exams are given Monday – Friday by Appointment Only. To schedule an appointment, contact the UofM Testing Center by calling 901.678.2428. For detailed information regarding the ParaPro Assessment, please visit the ParaPro Assessment website: What to Bring A non-expired Government issued photo ID is required and must be presented prior to testing. Your photo ID must contain a signature and match name you used when registering exactly - NO EXCEPTIONS. Listed below are the only acceptable forms of photo identification: State-issued driver's license, State-issued ID card, Passport, Military ID card Bring printed payment receipt sent to you by Marketplace ( be sure to check your spam/junk folder if you are unable to locate the receipt in your inbox ). The exam fee is due on the day of your scheduled exam. The fee is payable by Credit/Debit card that displays the MasterCard, Visa or American Express logo.
