This request is only valid for students who currently have a University of Memphis housing/meal plan charge. Beginning Monday, January 4, log in to your Campus Card account to request your Dining Dollars be applied to your housing/meal plan charge. After submitting your transfer request online, you will receive a confirmation email. The amount requested (or verified Dining Dollars balance) will be credited to your account balance on TigerXpress. Credits will be applied during the work week - usually within forty eight hours after you submit your request. You will then be able to pay your account balance in full or sign up and pay on the Installment Payment Plan on TigerXpress. Please Note: Students whose fees were paid in full with financial aid/scholarships/third party assistance and have requested Dining Dollars to be applied to their housing/meal plan charge will be refunded by January 29, 2016.
