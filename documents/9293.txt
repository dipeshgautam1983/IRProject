Campus Card Your Campus Card Dining Dollars Tiger Funds Meal Plans/Flex Bucks Dining Dollars Program Dining Dollars Refund Dining Dollars Transfer to Housing/Meal Plan Home Campus Card Dining Dollars Program Dining Dollars Refund Dining Dollars Refund The Dining Dollars Refund Period for Fall 2015 is Monday, November 30, 2015 through Friday, December 11, 2015. Any unused Dining Dollars will remain on your Dining Dollars account unless you request a refund during this specified period. Beginning Monday, November 30, log in to your Campus Card account (click Your Campus Card on the navigation menu above) to request your Dining Dollars refund. Select the Click Here to Refund My Dining Dollars button (made available on Monday, November 30). Refund requests must be submitted online by Friday, December 11, 2015. If you submit your refund request correctly, you will receive an e-mail confirmation. If you do not receive and an e-mail confirmation within 30 minutes, e-mail campuscard@memphis.edu for verification. Dining Dollars will be available for spending through Friday, December 11, 2015. Refunds: Refunds will be completed by Friday, December 18, 2015. eRefunds will begin to post to accounts Friday, December 18, 2015. Checks will be mailed Friday, December 18, 2015. Students are responsible for verifying and/or updating their eRefund account information on TigerXpress as well as their mailing address on the myMemphis portal. Refunds will be processed to the student's eRefund (direct deposit) account through TigerXpress. If no refund payment profile exists in TigerXpress, a check will be sent to the mailing address on file with the University. Sign up for eRefund on TigerXpress . If you request a refund and your balance is $5.00 or less, your balance will remain on your Dining Dollars account. Please note that any amount owed to the University will be deducted from your available balance in the account prior to the processing of a refund.
