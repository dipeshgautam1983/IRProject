A stolen laptop can lead to loss of important data, or even identity theft. Below are a few ways you can keep your laptop physically safe. Don’t leave your laptop in your car. Don’t store passwords with your laptop. Don’t put your laptop on the floor. (You might forget it’s there) Don’t leave your laptop unattended. Use a password for logging in. Use a laptop lock. Write down the serial number for your laptop. Carry your laptop in a discreet bag. Back up your data. (Keep your backup separate from your laptop) If your laptop has been stolen report it immediately to the appropriate authorities. If you fear someone may be misusing your personal information visit ftc.gov/idtheft . For more information about laptop security, click here .
