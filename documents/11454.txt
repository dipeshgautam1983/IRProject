Welcome to the University of Memphis Libraries Zoom H4n Guide! Students at the University of Memphis are can check out a Zoom H4n portable audio recorder from the circulation desk in the Music Library, pursuant to the following guidelines: 1. One recorder per student. (There are two available.) 2. Check out for three days ($1.00/hour late fee). 3. There are no renewals. You can check it out again after 24 hours. 4. Make sure you follow the instructions for usage and refer to this guide. 5. Be sure to save your recordings to your computer or use your own memory card. The Libraries are not responsible for any content left on the provided memory card upon check-in. Want some training? Attend a workshop !
