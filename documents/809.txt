Organization &amp; Employee Development Staff Directory Info &amp; Programs New Employee UMBoarding Human Resources Home Organization &amp; Employee Development UMBoarding Welcome to the University of Memphis UMboarding! We are excited that you have chosen the University of Memphis to continue your professional career and want to make sure you have a smooth transition as you join the University's team. UMboarding is a great opportunity for you to learn about the University's mission, vision and core values as well as all the services and benefits that are available to you as an employee. We encourage you to peruse our site and the links below to get acquainted with the many areas within the University that will assist with your transition. It is our hope that as you browse our site you will develop appreciation for the University of Memphis community and culture, and familiarize yourself with University resources and other amenities. If you need additional information or have questions, please contact the Department of Human Resources, Organization &amp; Employee Development Office, at 901.678.3575 located in 177 Administration Building. Once again, we welcome you to the University of Memphis and look forward to being a part of your continued success as you join our team. There are three phases of UMboarding: Phase I: New Employee Orientation During the face-to-face New Employee Orientation session, you will be able to meet other new employees as well as learn about the University's history, its strategic plan and how you will play an intricate part in moving the University towards becoming a top research Institution. Amidst the course of the day, Workforce Management, Employee Safety and Health, and Employee Benefits render pertinent information to ready you for your benefits coverage and overall campus safety and awareness. Phase II: On-boarding Event Approximately within three months of hiring, we invite all new employees to share their experiences with us. Phase III: Management Talk On a quarterly basis, the management team of new employees is brought together to share the UMboarding process, the experience of new employees, and the expectations for them to assist new employees in the University's culture.
