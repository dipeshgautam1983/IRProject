University Benefits Administration Staff Directory Benefits Information Related Resources &amp; Links Human Resources Home University Benefits Administration Benefits Information For Employees in Same-Sex Marriages Benefits Information For Employees in Same-Sex Marriages Based on the Supreme Court ruling on the Constitutional right to same-sex marriage effective June 26, 2015, the University Benefits Administration Office is accepting and processing insurance applications from legally-married, same-sex couples. Applicants will have 60 days to apply from the later of: the marriage date, or the date of the Supreme Court ruling – June 26, 2015 (for those whose marriages occurred prior to the ruling in states where the marriage was legally authorized) Applications will be processed according to the same standards used for all married couples, which includes documentation requirements, notifications, etc. If the application is outside the initial eligibility period, or if the employee is requesting to add other eligible dependents along with the spouse, the usual rules regarding enrollment by special qualifying event or open enrollment will apply. Additional information on eligibility and enrollment can be found in the Plan Document . The Enrollment Change Application is available online . Applications and supporting documents can be delivered to the University Benefits Administration Office in 165 Administration Building or faxed to 901.678.1650. Coverage effective dates: As stated on the enrollment change application, for those adding a new dependent-spouse, the effective date of coverage is the date of marriage OR first day of the month following marriage. For those applicants who have 60 days from the date of the Supreme Court ruling to apply for benefits, the coverage is effective on the date of the decision (June 26, 2015). The applicant would have to pay the full month's premium for the added spouse and/or dependents; or, coverage can be made effective on July 1, 2015. Members can also add eligible spouses and dependents during the annual enrollment period in the fall. The annual enrollment period this year is September 15 – October 15. Coverage selected during the annual enrollment period will be in effect January 1, 2016. If you have questions, please contact the University Benefits Administration Office at benefits@memphis.edu .
