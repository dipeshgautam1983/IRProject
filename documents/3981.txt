Manufacturing Technology Management Why choose Manufacturing Technology Management? Most manufacturing companies expect univ ersity graduates to demonstrate critical thinking, problem solving, anal ytical and business communicati on skills necessary for their continued success. A major in Manufacturing Management sets our graduates up with the right set of skills and knowledge for whatever manufacturing environmen t they face. These advanced skills will come from focused areas with proven faculty. Th ey are manufacturing technology, management, supervisory, production control and facility de sign. You will gain experi ence from becoming a better speaker, manager and developing leadership skills for the industry of your choice! Our manufacturing technology management degree th rough University College will help you develop your ability to problem solve, communicate and motivate through action in your coursework. Skills like these will make you one of the top sought after Manufacturing Technology Management graduates in West Tennessee. Are you someone who can better maneuver a difficult economy and make it better by addi ng your expertise and skill sets? Manufacturing Management majors may find em ployment in any area of the United States. However the most critical need is one we have here in Tennessee. Come take a look at the University College Bachelor of Professional Studies (BPS) in Manufacturing Technology Management. You will be well prepared for a variety of careers such as: Facility Design Corporate Management Product Marketing Manager Process Development Human Resources Plant Leader Logistics Manager Safety and Compliance Manager Lean Production Manager Operations Manager Production Manager Manufacturing Supervisor Sales and Marketing in Manufacturing Cohorts starting Fall 2015 at The University of Memphis Lambuth.
