Fogelman College - MBA Programs Welcome Programs Students Faculty &amp; Staff Research News FCBE Home MBA Programs MBA Programs Interested in better career prospects or want to advance in your career? The University of Memphis offers six different MBA tracks. Choose the one that is appropriate for you. Professional MBA Want to get an MBA degree at your own pace? Want to specialize in a particular functional area? The Professional MBA is right for you. Online MBA Have a traveling job and want an MBA degree? Like the flexibility of taking courses from home? The Online MBA is right for you. International MBA Ready for a career in a Global organization? Like studying abroad and understanding other cultures? The International MBA is right for you. Executive MBA Want to go from the class room to the board room? Prefer to attend classes with the top executives in Memphis? The Executive MBA is right for you. Customer Driven MBA Enjoy interning while you study? Like an MBA being sponsored by a Fortune 500 Corporation? The CD MBA is right for you. Entrepreneurship MBA Have a great idea for a start-up company? This MBA track is designed for aspiring entrepreneurs and designed to help you launch your own business. Which University of Memphis MBA? Regardless of where you are in your career or life, and whether you live across the street or across the world we have an MBA right for you.
