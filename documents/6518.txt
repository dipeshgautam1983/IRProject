Outlook provides a clear, unified view of emails, calendars and contacts to keep your information and communication organized. Stay connected and go beyond basic email tasks to get more done like journaling and document sharing. Access umMail on a PC, Mac or Mobile Device. Getting Started Use the Getting Started guide if you have questions on how to begin using your umMail account. To access your email, login using your UUID (uuid@memphis.edu) and your account password. If you have forgotten your account, or need additional assistance, please contact the umTech Service Desk at 901.678-8888. Forgot your password? Reset it using the self-service option at iAM . Once you've successfully accessed your umMail account, you can now sync your email with your mobile device. Currently, you can use either an IOS, Android or Blackberry device. Please visit AskTOM for more details. Also, now available: Office 365 for Education! It is an Office 365 plan that is available for free to active students, faculty and staff. This plan allows you to install Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote, Publisher, and Access on up to 5 PCs or Macs and Office apps on other mobile devices including Windows tablets and iPads. Get your free download at Get Office .
