Support the FCBE News Dean's Message Giving Stories Giving Options Development FCBE Home Support the FCBE Welcome Thank you for your consideration of an investment in the Fogelman College of Business &amp; Economics. We are honored! It is because of your investments of your dollars and your time that we continue to have a positive impact on our students and our business community. As an alumnus/na or friend of the college you can partner with us to provide an impact in numerous ways: You can invest in the college by making a tax-deductible gift . Your investments support endowed scholarships, graduate assistantships, faculty instruction, building renovations, and professional development, real-world application, internships, and leadership opportunities for our students. You may direct your gift to any area within the Fogelman College. Serve on an advisory board. Your experience is invaluable and can help our various schools within the Fogelman College connect with important businesses and people. Provide internships for our students. The Complete Professional Program through the Avron Fogelman Professional Development Center can help you select the ideal intern for your company. Speak to classes on your career development and business knowledge. Sharing your expertise with students is the most direct way to impact students' career development. Mentor students. Through classroom participation or one-on-one mentoring, our students seek and require your network, influence, and experience. Hire our graduates. With your engagement and gifts we can enrich our college and support our community. Alumni and friends are the difference between a good and great university. I encourage you to contact me with your interest to become more involved in any fashion with the Fogelman College of Business &amp; Economics. I can be reached at M.Hartquist@memphis.edu or 901.678.3068. Best, Marianne Hartquist Sr. Director of Development Fogelman College of Business &amp; Economics Make a Gift to Fogelman College Today!
