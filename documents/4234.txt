For more information about joining an Intramurals or Club Sports team, call our Intramurals Office at 678.2802 or 678.2812. 1. All team rosters must be created &amp; submitted on , no later than 11:59 pm on the deadline day. 2. All Managers' Meetings are mandatory and will be held in the Student Recreation and Fitness Center at 5:30 pm in the Racquetball Bleachers. 3. If your team is not represented at the manager meeting you may not qualify for the post-season tournament and must pay a $30 forfeit fine for not representing your team at the manager's meeting. 4. No entries will be accepted after the deadline unless spaces are available. 5. All sports are free to enter except golf. 6. All times and dates are subject to change.
