The UofM Alumni Association Membership Get Involved Events News Tiger Pride Support UofM About Membership Join Now Services &amp; Benefits List of Benefits Email365 Insurance Travel New Grads Students Pouncer's Pals Home UMAA Membership New Grads New Grads : Associate Members Congratulations on your recent graduation from the University of Memphis! As a new graduate, the Alumni Association is pleased to present you with a complimentary one year membership. You will receive your membership card with your diploma. Your connection to the University does not end upon graduation. Participation in Alumni Association activities not only helps your alma mater grow stronger but makes your degree more valuable. We hope you stay connected to your University by getting involved with some of the more than 70 alumni clubs, chapters and programs based on academics, geography or a specific interest. Moving after graduation? Join a club in your area! Joining the Young Alumni is a perfect way to network with fellow alumni! Click here for a complete list of Alumni Clubs and Chapters. We hope to see you at an event soon! You also receive benefits as an Alumni Association member! Click here for a list of benefits that are available to you. Some that are particularly great for new grads include: Email365 Rec Center membership discount Discounts on textbooks (if you're returning to the UofM for grad school) Insurance Discounts Connect with your Alumni Association and fellow Tigers on our Facebook , Twitter and Instagram pages. And don't forget to update your information so you can stay up-to-date on news and upcoming events! Questions about your membership? Contact the Alumni Association at alumniassociation@memphis.edu or 901.678.ALUM (2586).
