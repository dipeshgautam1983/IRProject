Faculty, Staff FREE Night at the Theatre Home Theatre &amp; Dance season Faculty Staff FREE Night at the Theatre The Department of Theatre &amp; Dance is very excited about all of the exciting things happening in our building and across campus in other departments. We would like to honor the hard work and dedication all U of M faculty and staff by offering a U of M Faculty and Staff FREE Night at the theatre. So, take a break and join us the first Friday of each Mainstage production. How to get your 2 FREE tickets any Faculty and Staff FREE Night You may reserve your tickets by calling the box office 678-2576 or by ordering them online here . To pick tickets up, please bring your faculty/staff id to the box office located in the Theatre Building on Central Ave. All tickets are available on a first come, first served basis. The fall 2015 box office hours are... Monday 11:30am to 4:00pm Tuesday 11:30am to 4:00pm Wednesday 9:00am to 4:00pm Thursday 9:00am to 4:00pm Friday 9:00am to 4:00pm The box office opens at 6:30 on performance nights. We ask that you pick up your tickets by 7:00pm on the night of the performance. If you have any questions, please call Alice Berry at 678-3184. Current Season Purchase Tickets Next UP!
