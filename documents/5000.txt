In order for the University to release these records, it must have your consent. What does your record include? See Inventory of Academic and Student Education Records . To give your consent: Complete and sign a Student Consent to Release Education Record Information form. You may complete the form in our office, or you may complete the form and FAX it to us. Provide a photo ID--driver's license, U of M ID card, etc.--as proof of your identity. If you make the request in our office, be sure to bring your ID with you. If you FAX or mail us a completed form, you must attach a photocopy of your picture ID. We cannot process your release request without proof of your identity. Bring, FAX, or mail all documents to: University of Memphis Assistant Registrar - Student Records 003 Wilder Tower Memphis, TN 38152-3520 FAX: 901.678.3249 Note : You may revoke your consent after you have given it. Bring a signed, written request to Student Records. Be sure you bring a photo ID (student ID or drivers license) as well.
