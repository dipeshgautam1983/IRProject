All tours begin in Wilder Tower, Room 101. You and your guests will check in at the Information Desk in the lobby of Wilder. For more information or to schedule a tour, send us an e-mail or call 800.669.2678 or 901.678.2169. You can also schedule a tour online by following the links below: Schedule your individual or small group tour (fewer than ten visitors) Schedule your group tour (groups of ten or more visitors) Please try to schedule at least two weeks in advance. Parking When you arrive on campus, please display the parking pass on your dash that you will receive with your tour confirmation letter. You may also park in the parking garage (for a fee), which is accessed from Zach Curlin Street, or visit Parking Services for a $2 visitor parking pass for the Southern Ave. or Central Ave. lots.
