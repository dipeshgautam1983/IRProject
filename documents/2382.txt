School of Health Studies About Programs Students Faculty Research Centers News Contact SHS Students Prospective Current Admissions &amp; Advising Internships Study Abroad Home SHS Students Admission &amp; Advising Admission &amp; Advising Undergraduate Admissions Graduate Admissions All students interested in declaring their major in a School of Health Studies program should set up an appointment with Kathy Walker (901-678-4316 or kwalker1@memphis.edu ). Students will need to follow the appropriate link above for specific admission instructions. Ms. Walker or her assistant will: Help all students through the admission process Advise all SHS undergraduate student majors Assign all SHS graduate students to a faculty advisor Assist with web registration and scheduling issues Explain the academic policies and regulations As a student, it is important that you play an active role in your advising, to ensure that you remain on track with your degree program schedule and to maximize the time that you have with your SHS advisor. For optimal success you must: Know you advisor (Ms.Walker or her assistant). Accept responsibility for academic choices. Know basic university, college, and departmental graduation requirements for your chosen major and concentration well enough to plan your own progress toward degree completion. Keep current on academic policies, procedures, and requirements by reviewing the current Undergraduate Bulletin, Schedule of Classes, and degree plans. Maintain personal copies of your tentative degree plan, progress reports, and transfer credit evaluations until the official graduation application is processed. Take the above materials to each advising conference. Develop social, academic, and career goals and examine how these goals will affect your life and the lives of persons important to you. See your advisor at least once a semester to plan your schedule of coursework, and to seek further help as needed.
