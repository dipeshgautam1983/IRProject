To obtain a username and password, please email lawcareerservices@memphis.edu with your name, graduation month/year, and your preferred email address. Please allow up to two business days for a response. Individual Career Counseling We invite Memphis Law alumni considering a job or career transition to make an appointment with our office via email l awcareerservices@memphis.edu . Appointments are held in the CSO for those in the Memphis area, or by telephone for those outside of the area. We will help you develop a job search strategy, research potential employers, learn successful networking techniques, and find the job you want. We also offer resume and cover letter critique via email. Send your materials to lawcareerservices@memphis.edu . Events and Workshops Memphis Law alumni are welcome to attend CSO workshops and presentations. You must register for such events through Symplicity.
