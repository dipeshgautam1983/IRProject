Campus Recreation Intramural Services About Membership Classes/Programs Aquatics Intramurals/Club Sports Facilities Aquatics Facilities and Programs Swim Lessons/Classes Early Bird Swim Program Pool Rules Home CRIS Aquatics Swim Lessons/Classes Swim Lessons/Classes Fall american red cross swim lessons Our swim classes offer a supportive, nurturing, and safe environment in which participants ages six months to adult can learn to swim or improve their existing skills. Group swim lessons are based on availability. Please come to entry desk at Recreation Center to register for group swim lessons. Class sessions will be held on Saturday mornings, Sept. 5-Oct. 31 (No classes on Oct. 24.). Times: Parent/Child (3 and under): 9:30-10 a.m. Preschool (4-5 years old): 10:15-10:45 a.m. Youth Beginners I (No previous water experience): 11-11:40 a.m. Youth Beginners II (already have mastery of basic water skills, including floating on front and back, swimming 15 feet without assistance, gliding, and kicking): 11-11:40 a.m. Youth Intermediate (Swims easily on stomach in deep water, breathes side to side, and can swim front and back crawl): 12-12:40 p.m. Adults and Teens (13 and up): 12-12:40 p.m. Cost: Students , $40; Members , $60; Non-Members , $80 Private Lessons Click here to register for private swim lessons. Sessions : Five lessons, 30 minutes each Cost: Students, $80, Members, $100, Non-Members, $120 At CRIS, we are passionate about teaching kids and adults to be strong swimmers. Our lessons are for swimmers from age four to adult and include a private one-on-one session with your very own instructor for extreme beginners or those who want to condition through swimming. For more information, please call 901.678.3461 or email Aquatic and Safety Coordinator Ashley Chism.
