Registrar About Register Students Veterans Faculty &amp; Staff Forms Home Registrar Calendars Spring 2016 Final Exams Spring 2016 Final Exams General Rules &amp; Guidelines Attendance is compulsory. Exams for Full Term classes that meet at a time not included in the schedule below should be given at a time as close to the regular class time as possible. Faculty teaching classes at non-standard times that overlap the exams for classes offered at standard times must make alternative arrangements with students who have conflicts. The department chair should approve these arrangements. No exam period, as scheduled below, may be changed without the written permission of the department chair and the college dean. Common final exams are given for certain courses and are noted in the Class Schedule Search .
