Department of Computer Science About Future Students Current Students Research People Contact Hiring News &amp; Events Department News Upcoming Events Colloquium Undergraduate Colloquium Newsletters Home Computer Science News &amp; Events Upcoming Outreach Events Upcoming Outreach Events Posted on 2015-11-17 The Department has several outreach events scheduled for the month of December. We will be holding a reception for newly admitted Computer Science freshmen on Thursday, Dec. 10 from 5:00-6:00 pm (venue TBA). The reception will include presentations on financial aid and opportunities for undergraduate students. If you received an invitation to this event, you can register online at . The annual Computer Science Open House will be Friday, Dec. 11 from 9:00 am-2:00 pm (venue TBA). This event aims to increase awareness of computer science in local middle and high schools, in celebration of the nationwide Computer Science Education Week . The Open House will allow students to see some examples of our faculty and student projects, as well as hear about interesting applications of computer science like video game design and robotics. If you'd like to bring a group from your local school, please register online at . Our next 100 Girls of Code workshop will be Saturday, Dec. 12 from 9:00 am-1:00 pm in Manning Hall 430/432. This free workshop will introduce girls ages 10-18 to computer science with holiday-themed drawing and animation lessons in JavaScript. Register by contacting Dr. Laney Strange at laneyd@gmail.com . We will host an Hour of Code event on Saturday, Dec. 12 from 9:30-11:00 am in Dunn Hall 118, based on the global Hour of Code initiative. Contact Dr. Marko Puljic for more information.
