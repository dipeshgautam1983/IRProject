Early Intervention This is a university - wide early alert program designed to inform students of help they may need for courses in which they are enrolled. Each fall and spring, faculty are encouraged to report students who are not attending or who are hav ing academic or other difficulties. This program is housed in the Center for Academic Retention and enhancement Services (CARES): After a report is submitted by a faculty member, the student receives an e - mail with suggestions for been entered on SGAADVR. The director of advising in our college receives a list of students who were reported the day before, which she sends to the appropriate advisors.
