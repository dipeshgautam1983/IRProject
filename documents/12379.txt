Registrar About Register Students Veterans Faculty &amp; Staff Forms Forms Forms Student Veterans Faculty/Staff Parents Former Lambuth University Students Home Registrar Forms Forms: Veterans Forms: Veterans Veterans Advance Pay Request (UofM form) Use this form to apply for advance payment. Complete and sign the form, then deliver it to Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification, 003 Wilder Tower, Memphis, TN 38152-3520. FAX: 901.678.1425. Visit Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification for more information. Veterans Drop/Withdrawal - Mitigating Circumstances (UofM form) Use this form if you drop courses or withdraw before the end of the term due to circumstances beyond your control. Complete and sign the form, then deliver it to Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification, 003 Wilder Tower, Memphis, TN 38152-3520. FAX: 901.678.1425. Visit Drops/Withdrawals - Mitigating Circumstances for more information. Veterans Request for Certification This form is web-based and requires that you have a UofM Username and Password. Use this form to register with the UofM's Office of Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification every semester in which you take classes. Visit Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification for more information. VA Education Forms Instructions and forms are available on the Veterans Affairs web site ; for additional information, visit UofM's Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification . Yellow Ribbon Request (UofM form) Use this form when registering for classes under Chapter 33, the Post-9/11 GI Bill® Yellow Ribbon Program. (GI Bill® is a registered trademark of the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). More information is available at the official U.S. government Web site at .) Complete and sign the form, then deliver it to Veterans Educational Benefits &amp; Certification, 003 Wilder Tower, Memphis, TN 38152-3520. FAX: 901.678.1425.
